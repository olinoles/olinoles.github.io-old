Hi, I'm Oliver

I'm an Auckland-based tech enthusiast experienced in web technologies, creative projects, and UI design.

I am most interested in full-stack development and enjoy working with both front-end and back-end tools. Outside of webdev, I enjoy hobbies that involve robotics and electronics, including 3D printing, building FPV Drones and CAD design.

My favourite projects currently involve developing clean and functional front-end software, but I'm not afraid of working on back-end applications either. My philosophy towards coding is the right tool for the job. Having a strong passion for learning new tools allows me to solve problems efficiently.

When I'm not working on projects I love to socialise with board games, try new craft beers, and travel.
